Why We Row

Both members of Landlocked Voyagers have lost family and friends to their struggles with mental health. We are very aware of how hard this struggle can be not only on the indivudual, but also on those around them. Along with AFSP, Landlocked Voyagers is commited to letting those that are struggling feel heard.
